Privacy & contact

What this site stores

  • Scientist entries — the writeups, sources, and photos shown on the public pages, plus a contributor name where one was provided.
  • Photos — stored on this server (not hot-linked). Where a photo's license isn't confirmed, it is labelled source unverified and links back to its origin.
  • When class collections launch: a teacher's name and optional email (used only to re-send their management link), and standard web-server logs including IP addresses (used for rate-limiting and abuse prevention).

Students & minors

Introductory physics is sometimes taught to under-18 students. This site is not directed at children under 13. When class collections launch, student work will be credited by first name and last initial by default, teachers will be able to require full anonymity, and teachers remain responsible for their school's consent and media-release policies.

Corrections & removal

To fix an error, update attribution, or request removal of a name or photo, contact the site operator and it will be handled promptly. Note that once material has been validly published under an open license, copies made by others cannot be recalled — but it will be removed from this site and its exports.

Where the data lives

The site runs on a single small server; backups are encrypted and stored off-site. There are no third-party trackers or advertising networks.
